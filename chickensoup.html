Tomato Soup

Description

Ingredients

  • Yellow onion
  • Chicken Sausage
  • Garlic
  • Dried Oregano
  1. Wash and dry all produce. Halve, peel, and finely chop onion until you have ¾ cup. Peel and finely chop garlic. Medium dice tomato.
  2. Heat a drizzle of oil in a large pot over medium-high heat. Add onion and season with salt and pepper. Cook, stirring, until softened, about 4 minutes. Add chicken sausage, garlic, and oregano. Cook, breaking up meat into pieces, until browned and cooked through, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add tomato and tomato paste to pot with sausage mixture. Cook, stirring, until tomato starts to break down, 2-3 minutes. Stir in 2 ½ cups water (4 cups for 4 servings), stock concentrates, and plenty of salt and pepper.
